When messy books stall a case

Tax resolution cases rarely arrive with clean financials. Clients come to your firm because they're already behind—and their books reflect it. Months or years of incomplete records, missing documentation, and unreliable data.

Before your team can negotiate with the IRS, prepare filings, or build a resolution strategy, someone has to reconstruct those books. That work often falls to internal staff who should be focused on higher-value case work.

RetroBooks exists to solve that bottleneck. We handle the historical bookkeeping reconstruction so your team can stay focused on what they do best—resolving cases and serving clients.
