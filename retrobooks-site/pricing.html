What determines final pricing

Every engagement is scoped individually. The starting price of $299 per month applies to standard reconstructions, but the final quote reflects the specific conditions of each case.

Complexity of the books

Simple single-entity books with standard transactions are priced differently than complex multi-entity structures with mixed revenue streams.

Available records

Engagements with complete bank statements and supporting documentation are more straightforward than cases with significant gaps in records.

Number of accounts

The number of bank accounts, credit cards, and other financial accounts that need to be reconciled affects the scope of the reconstruction work.

Accounting platform and records format

The format and state of existing records—whether in QuickBooks, Xero, spreadsheets, or paper—can influence the scope of the reconstruction work.

Common questions about pricing

Is there a minimum engagement size?

No. We handle engagements as small as a few months and as large as multi-year reconstructions. Pricing is per month of books reconstructed.

Are there any retainers or long-term commitments?

No. RetroBooks operates on a per-engagement basis. You bring us cases as they arise. No monthly retainers, no annual contracts.

What if the scope changes during the engagement?

If we discover additional complexity or missing records that affect the scope, we communicate the revised estimate before proceeding. No surprise invoices.

Is white-label service priced differently?

White-label engagements follow the same pricing structure. The capability to present the work under your firm's brand is included.

Need a quote for a specific case?

Share the case details and we'll provide a scoped quote based on the months of backlog and complexity involved.
